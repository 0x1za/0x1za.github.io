## Research
Focused on **Computer Vision** and **OCR**. I explore how to make models resilient to the entropy of the real world. My publications are available on [Scholar](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=soZWeewAAAAJ&hl=en).

## Engineering
In my daily work, I build across **Java**, **Ruby**, and **Python**. In my spare time, I am exploring the deterministic and expressive worlds of **Rust** and **Clojure**.

## Philosophy
In computational theory, we often ignore the "constant factor." In engineering, the constant factor is where the user lives. This site is a place to document the **Big-O** of my research and the **constant factors** of my engineering.
